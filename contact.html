Introduction

Hello! I explore how technology, especially artificial intelligence, impacts us humans. The podcast has a wide range of guests, from technical to legal experts, scientists, and industry leaders. No prior AI knowledge is needed. While discussions are in-depth and with experts, we always try to explain everything from the ground up and connect it to issues of relevance for today and our future as a society.

Some of the questions we look into are:

  • Why doctors outperform AI in healthcare realistic scenarios? (Episode 10: Dr. Artin Entezarjou)
  • What if we focused on balance rather than maximization narrow objectives in AI design? (Episode 9: Diederik Roijers)
  • How can philosophy, psychology, and law inform AI design? (Episode 8: Mehdi Dastani)
  • What does it take to make real-time decisions in high-stakes environments and how to deal with uncertainty? (Episode 7: Albert Podusenko and İsmail Şenöz)
  • What are AI's ethical and legal challenges? (Episodes 4 with Colette Cuijpers and 6 with Inge Brattinga)
  • How ideas from macroevolution, extinction, and competition inform our undestanding of AI? (Episodes 5 with Indre Žliobaitė)
  • Why can't current AI truly understand cause and effect and how to fix that? (Episode 3: Alexander Molak)

Join me as I discuss innovation, human-centered design, and the future of technology with experts, scientists, and leaders.
